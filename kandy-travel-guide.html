Introduction

Kandy Travel Guide: Top Attractions, Things to Do & Travel Tips

Kandy is one of the most important cultural cities in Sri Lanka and a must-visit destination for travelers exploring the island. Located in the central highlands, Kandy is known for its rich history, beautiful landscapes, and religious significance. Surrounded by mountains and centered around a peaceful lake, the city offers a unique blend of culture, nature, and tradition.

From the famous Temple of the Tooth Relic to scenic viewpoints and cultural performances, Kandy provides a memorable experience for every traveler. This Kandy travel guide will help you explore the best places to visit, things to do, and useful tips for your journey as part of wider Sri Lanka tour packages.

Why Visit

Why Visit Kandy

Kandy is a cultural and spiritual hub in Sri Lanka. It is one of the best places to experience traditional Sri Lankan heritage, festivals, and local life. The city's cool climate and scenic surroundings make it a relaxing destination compared to coastal areas.

Kandy is ideal for:

  • cultural travelers
  • families and couples
  • history lovers
  • nature enthusiasts
  • first-time visitors to Sri Lanka
Best Experiences

Top Things to Do in Kandy

Temple of the Tooth Relic

The Temple of the Tooth Relic is the most famous attraction in Kandy. It houses a sacred relic of Buddha and is one of the most important religious sites in Sri Lanka. Visitors can experience daily rituals and explore the temple complex.

Kandy Lake

Kandy Lake is located in the heart of the city and offers a peaceful place for a walk. The lake is surrounded by scenic views and is perfect for relaxing and enjoying the atmosphere.

Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya

These gardens are among the most beautiful in Sri Lanka, featuring a wide variety of plants, orchids, and trees. It is a great place for nature lovers and families.

Cultural Dance Shows

Kandy is famous for its traditional dance performances. Visitors can enjoy cultural shows featuring Kandyan dancing, fire walking, and drumming.

Kandy View Point

This viewpoint offers panoramic views of the city, lake, and surrounding hills. It is a great spot for photography, especially during sunset.

Nearby Highlights

Best Places to Visit Near Kandy

Nuwara Eliya

Located a few hours from Kandy, Nuwara Eliya is known for its tea plantations, cool climate, and scenic beauty. It is also a popular onward route for travelers heading to the Ella travel guide part of their itinerary.

Knuckles Mountain Range

This mountain range is ideal for trekking, nature exploration, and adventure activities.

Victoria Dam

Victoria Dam is a beautiful location offering scenic views and peaceful surroundings.

Best Time to Go

Best Time to Visit Kandy

Kandy can be visited throughout the year, but the best time depends on weather conditions and festivals.

Timing Tips
  • December to April offers pleasant weather
  • August is famous for the Kandy Esala Perahera festival
  • early mornings and evenings are best for sightseeing
  • avoid heavy rainy periods for outdoor activities
Getting There

How to Reach Kandy

Kandy is well connected to major cities in Sri Lanka. It is easily accessible by road and rail.

Ways to reach Kandy:

  • train from Colombo (scenic journey)
  • private car or taxi
  • bus from different parts of the country
  • guided tour packages
Travel Tips

Travel Tips for Kandy

To enjoy your visit to Kandy, consider these tips:

  • dress modestly when visiting temples
  • remove shoes before entering religious sites
  • carry water and wear comfortable clothing
  • visit early to avoid crowds
  • respect local customs and traditions
  • keep your belongings safe in crowded areas
