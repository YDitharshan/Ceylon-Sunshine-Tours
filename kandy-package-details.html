Tour
Kandy

Kandy, Central Province
1 day, 1 night

Kandy is a large city in central Sri Lanka. It's set on a plateau surrounded by mountains, which are home to tea plantations and biodiverse rainforest. The city's heart is scenic Kandy Lake (Bogambara Lake), which is popular for strolling. Kandy is famed for sacred Buddhist sites, including the Temple of the Tooth (Sri Dalada Maligawa) shrine, celebrated with the grand Esala Perahera annual procession.

Destination Kandy, Central Province
Duration 1 Day / 1 Night
Schedule All Time

Discover the cultural capital of Sri Lanka with the Kandy Tour by Ceylon Sunshine Tours. Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, explore the beautiful Royal Botanical Garden, climb the famous Ambuluwawa Tower, experience a traditional Tea Factory, and enjoy scenic waterfalls along the journey.

Includes

  • Comfortable air-conditioned transportation
  • Professional English speaking driver guide
  • Visit to the Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Explore Royal Botanical Garden Peradeniya
  • Climb the famous Ambuluwawa Tower viewpoint
  • Visit to traditional tea factory and plantation
  • Stop at beautiful scenic waterfalls
  • Photo stops at mountain viewpoints
  • City sightseeing in Kandy
  • Complimentary bottled drinking water
