Tour
Sigiriya-Dumbulla

Tours depart from Pasikuda, Batticaloa (East Coast)

Matale, Central Province
1 day

Sigiriya or Sinhagiri is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province, Sri Lanka. It is a site of historical and archaeological significance that is dominated by a massive column of granite approximately 180 m high.

Destination Matale, Central Province
Duration 1 Day
Schedule All Time

Experience one of the most popular cultural journeys in Sri Lanka with our Sigiriya & Dambulla Day Tour. This tour takes you through the heart of Sri Lanka’s cultural triangle, where history, nature, and village life come together.

Climb the world-famous Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient frescoes, mirror wall, and breathtaking views from the summit. Visit the sacred Dambulla Cave Temple, the largest and best-preserved cave temple complex in Sri Lanka, filled with beautiful Buddha statues and colorful wall paintings.
Enjoy an authentic Sri Lankan village tour, where you can experience traditional rural life including bullock cart rides and local cooking demonstrations. The adventure continues with an exciting elephant safari, where you can observe elephants and wildlife in their natural surroundings.

Includes

  • Comfortable air-conditioned transportation
  • Professional English speaking driver guide
  • Hotel pickup and drop-off service
  • Visit to Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Visit to Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Traditional Sri Lankan village tour experience
  • Elephant safari experience
  • Visit to herbal and spice garden
  • Shopping stop for local handicrafts and souvenirs
  • Complimentary bottled drinking water
